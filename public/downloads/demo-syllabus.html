Curriculum Scope

Structured learning for every stage

The academic program combines core subjects, moral development, communication skills, digital literacy, and practical classroom engagement. Each section is planned to support both exam performance and confident growth.

Primary Section

Class I to V
  • Foundational Bangla, English, Mathematics, and General Science.
  • Reading habits, handwriting, spoken confidence, and creative activities.
  • Continuous classwork, oral participation, and term-based evaluation.

Junior Secondary

Class VI to VIII
  • Stronger subject depth in language, mathematics, science, social studies, and ICT.
  • Project-based assignments and routine-based exam preparation.
  • Balanced focus on discipline, teamwork, and presentation skills.

Secondary Program

Class IX to X
  • Board-aligned syllabus with stream-based academic guidance.
  • Model tests, revision cycles, lab sessions, and performance tracking.
  • Career awareness and guardian communication before public exams.

Higher Secondary

Class XI to XII
  • Advanced content coverage with academic mentoring and exam discipline.
  • Focused preparation through chapter completion targets and test routines.
  • University readiness, responsibility, and independent study habits.
Academic Framework

Subjects, assessment, and learning rhythm

Core Subject Areas

  • Bangla Language and Literature
  • English Language and Composition
  • Mathematics and Applied Problem Solving
  • General Science / Physics / Chemistry / Biology
  • Bangladesh and Global Studies
  • ICT, Moral Education, Arts, and Physical Education

Assessment Pattern

Term Focus Weight
Class Tests Weekly and chapter review 20%
Half-Yearly Syllabus completion checkpoint 30%
Project / Practical Application and presentation 10%
Annual Exam Final mastery evaluation 40%
Co-curricular integration Debate, recitation, science fair participation, assembly speaking, and supervised club activities are included to strengthen confidence, communication, and leadership.
